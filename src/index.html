The beggining of the path.

I still remember creating my first website.

No revenue, no traffic, no business. None of that mattered.

It took me weeks to finally figure out how to launch my site.

Figuring out how web hosts worked, how to install WordPress, how to set everything up, buying a domain and pointing it to my website, the steps seemed endless.

After weeks of trial and error, I finally launched my first site.

That moment was the beginning of everything. It led me to build a blog, start freelancing, join tech startups, and build my own company.

Some of us at Quick Sprout have bought and sold companies, built blogs to millions of visitors per month, built email lists of 700,000 people, raised money for VC-backed startups, grown businesses to seven figures, and built products that customers loved.

None of it quite compares to building that first web site. That’s when we all got hooked. We love building and growing websites, it’s why we keep coming back.


Our mission at Quick Sprout is to help you get hooked too. Whether you’re creating your very first website, getting your first visitors, or scaling to the next level, we want to make sure you have every guide that you need along the way. That’s Quick Sprout.

Today, Quick Sprout is operated by a small group of online entrepreneurs, led by myself, Lars Lofgren.

We’re here to help you grow your traffic, business, and marketing career. Whether you’re a founder, freelancer, marketing manager, or executive, there’s something here for you.
